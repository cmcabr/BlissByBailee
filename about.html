BlissByBailee is a teen-run small business started in August 2020, and based on Etsy. BlissByBailee was born out of quarantine boredom when new hobbies were blooming. A small craft obsession bloomed into a self producing business, BlissByBailee. BlissByBailee sells hand-crafted accessories, such as rings, bags, apparel, and other jewelry items. BlissByBailee also started a joint podcast, Small Biz Big Ideas, which you can find on Spotify here.
