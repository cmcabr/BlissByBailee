What is BlissByBailee?

BlissByBailee is a teen-run small business started in August 2020, and based on Etsy. BlissByBailee was born out of quarantine boredom when new hobbies were blooming. A small craft obsession bloomed into a self producing business, BlissByBailee. BlissByBailee sells hand-crafted accessories, such as rings, bags, apparel, and other jewelry items. BlissByBailee was created with the purpose of showcasing affordable items for everyday wear that would be suitable to many different styles. You can shop BlissByBailee accessories on Instagram or Etsy, both linked below!

BlissByBailee also started a joint podcast, Small Biz Big Ideas, which you can find on Spotify here. If you are unable to attend the webinar, the Small Biz Big Ideas Podcast has 2 complete seasons of teen entrepreneurs interviewing other teen entrepreneurs to get their side of the business world. Tune in for loads of helpful information to expand your business knowledge!
