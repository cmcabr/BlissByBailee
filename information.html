Learn the basics of Entrepreneurship!



This webinar serves the purpose to educate young minds on the art of entrepenurship. Teens will be shown the many factors of entrepreneurship; such as financing, marketing,and communication, and taught how to master them. Taught by teens, for teens, this program is designed to allow a first-hand perspective into business as a teen. The webinar will last approximately 3 hours, from 10:00am CST to 1:00pm CST.
